# Volume XVI - Governance, RFC, Release, and Stewardship

## Governance Process

- Changes begin as issues or RFCs.
- Requirements update before or with implementation.
- Commander audit reviews major changes.
- Releases include validation evidence and certification status.

## Inputs and Outputs

Inputs are change proposals, requirements, implementation changes, tests, and
audit records. Outputs are accepted RFCs, release notes, and certification
updates.

## Preconditions and Postconditions

Change authority must be explicit. Deprecated behavior must include migration
guidance.

## Failure Modes

Unreviewed change, undocumented exception, release without evidence, or
incompatible policy migration.

## Latency and Resources

Governance workflows are process-bound. Automation should validate traceability
before release.

## Invariants

- No release without validation evidence.
- No silent governance exception.

## Traceability

Requirements: CBEP-004.

## Commander Audit

Stage 1 governance process is documented. External councils and public
interoperability programs are future work.

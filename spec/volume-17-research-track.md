# Volume XVII - Research Track and Future Evolution

## Research Areas

- Formal verification coverage.
- Federated audit reconciliation.
- Policy-language decidability.
- Human authorization UX.
- High-assurance deployment profiles.
- Interoperability and evidence repositories.

## Inputs and Outputs

Inputs are open questions, experiments, independent reviews, and benchmark
data. Outputs are research reports, accepted changes, and retired assumptions.

## Preconditions and Postconditions

Research must not silently mutate production guarantees. Experimental features
must be isolated.

## Failure Modes

Speculative feature adoption, unverifiable claims, benchmark overfitting, and
security regression.

## Latency and Resources

Research work has separate budgets and must not be mixed with runtime SLOs.

## Invariants

- Experimental work is marked.
- Evidence updates supersede assumptions.
- Commander audit records promotion from research to production.

## Traceability

Requirements: CBEP-004.
